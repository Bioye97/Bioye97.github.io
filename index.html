Welcome to Ajala Lab

We conduct interdisciplinary research that integrates continuum mechanics, math, computer science, and advances in instrumentation to push the boundaries of earth system science while informing natural hazard assessment. Our current research areas include Southern California, Indo-Burma, East and South Africa, and Madagascar.

Check out the research page to see some of our previous peer-reviewed work, and contact Rasheed if you want to team up on any exciting research idea.
